About GazaAid50

GazaAid50 unites crypto enthusiasts and humanitarian supporters under one banner. Our mission is to leverage the power of decentralized finance to deliver real‑world impact. We celebrate community‑driven initiatives and launch charity‑aligned projects like the $GAZA token. Half of all net profits are earmarked for organizations providing relief to Gaza.

Our Mission

1. Mobilise global supporters to fund humanitarian causes.
2. Build transparent, community‑driven crypto projects.
3. Donate 50% of net profits to relief efforts.
