About GazaAid 50 Token

GazaAid 50 ($GAZA) is a community‑driven Solana token created to funnel half of all net profits into humanitarian relief for Gaza. Launch details and tokenomics are still being finalised—watch this space for the official announcement.

Two Ways You Can Help

  1. Donate directly: Skip the middle‑layer and give straight to our four partner NGOs on the Donate page.
  2. Profit‑split wallet (coming soon): After launch, 50% of net profits will be deposited into a public Phantom wallet (24/7 live balance). During scheduled livestreams the entire balance will be distributed—25% each—to those same four organisations.

Launch & Liquidity

When the $GAZA token launches on 11 Aug 2025 at 4:00 PM CT, the total supply will be capped at 1 billion tokens. To promote a healthy ecosystem, 10% of the supply will be burned at launch. Initial liquidity will be provided with 1 SOL, and the full contract address (CA), audit and liquidity‑lock details will be published here.

Check back on launch day for the contract address and trading link.
