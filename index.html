Our Purpose

This project invites anyone to support humanitarian relief in Gaza while they earn. By holding the $GAZA token you can profit, and we encourage everyone who gains to donate 50 % of those profits to our partner NGOs. Engage, contribute ideas—this is a transparent community effort, not a scam or rug‑pull.
