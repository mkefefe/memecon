Our Impact & Giving Plan

Every purchase you make at MemeCon and every MemeCoin transaction helps us support vital humanitarian efforts. We’ve committed to donating 50 % of all profits to organizations working on the ground in Gaza. Here’s how your support will help:

Palestine Children’s Relief Fund (PCRF)

PCRF’s Gaza Relief and Recovery campaign supplies medical aid, treatment, food, clean water and necessities to affected families. They’re rebuilding healthcare facilities and funding trauma counseling and mental‑health services.

Donate

Save the Children

Save the Children delivers food, hygiene kits and clean drinking water, treats malnutrition, operates health centers and provides education and psychosocial support to children in Gaza.

Donate

Doctors Without Borders (MSF)

MSF provides surgical care, physiotherapy, primary health care, vaccinations, mental‑health services and critical water and sanitation activities through local and international teams in Gaza.

Donate

World Food Programme (WFP)

WFP warns that more than half a million people in Gaza face famine‑like conditions and over 320,000 children are at risk of acute malnutrition. They are ready to deliver 30,000 metric tons of food each month into Gaza once safe access is granted.

Donate
